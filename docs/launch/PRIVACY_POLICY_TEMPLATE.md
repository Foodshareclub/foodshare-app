# Privacy Policy Template for FoodShare

**Last Updated: February 11, 2026**

## Introduction

FoodShare ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, profile photo
- **Location Data**: Your location to show nearby food listings
- **Contact Information**: For communication between users
- **User Content**: Photos, descriptions, and messages you post

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, time spent, interactions
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, error logs

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the FoodShare service
- Show you nearby food listings
- Enable communication between users
- Send notifications about your activity
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Prevent fraud and ensure safety
- Comply with legal obligations

## Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share information with:

### Other Users
- Your public profile (name, photo, ratings)
- Food listings you post
- Messages you send to other users

### Service Providers
- Cloud hosting (Supabase)
- Analytics (if enabled)
- Crash reporting (Sentry)
- Push notifications

### Legal Requirements
- When required by law
- To protect rights and safety
- In connection with legal proceedings

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and data
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Control location sharing
- Export your data

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication
- Regular security audits
- Access controls

## Data Retention

We retain your information:
- While your account is active
- As needed to provide services
- To comply with legal obligations
- For up to 90 days after account deletion

## Children's Privacy

FoodShare is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Location Data

We collect location data to:
- Show nearby food listings
- Calculate distances
- Provide directions

You can control location permissions in your device settings.

## Third-Party Services

We use third-party services:
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication
- **Google Maps**: Location services
- **Sentry**: Error tracking (optional)

These services have their own privacy policies.
